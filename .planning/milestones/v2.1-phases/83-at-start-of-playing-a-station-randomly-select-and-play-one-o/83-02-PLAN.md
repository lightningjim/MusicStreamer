SomaFM importer extension. Adds (1) `preroll_urls` capture in `fetch_channels` (one-line addition to the channel dict at soma_import.py:234); (2) per-channel preroll insertion in `import_stations` (loops `ch["preroll_urls"]` calling `repo.insert_preroll`); (3) `repo.set_prerolls_fetched_at(station_id, int(time.time()))` once per imported channel regardless of `preroll_urls` count (D-04 marker semantics); (4) all three new operations land INSIDE the existing per-channel try/except rollback window BEFORE the `inserted_station_id = None` sentinel clear (Pitfall 4 mitigation); (5) per-channel cap of 50 preroll URLs (DoS hardening; aligns with Plan 83-01 `insert_preroll` cap; excess URLs are silently dropped with a `_log.warning`, channel still imports); (6) `tests/test_soma_import.py` appendix covering the new dict shape, insert calls, empty-preroll marker, per-channel cap, and CASCADE-via-rollback behavior.
