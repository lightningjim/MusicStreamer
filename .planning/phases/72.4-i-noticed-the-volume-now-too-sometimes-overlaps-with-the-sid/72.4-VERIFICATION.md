**Why human:** Pixel-perfect overlap assertion across font-rendering and Qt-style backends is fragile in headless test environments. The user's small lower display is the exact reproducer surface.

#### 2. Volume-Drag-Across-Resize Continuity

**Test:** (1) Begin dragging the volume slider thumb. (2) While dragging, widen the panel back past the threshold so the cluster returns to row 1. (3) Slider should remain grabbed; volume keeps updating; no Python tracebacks. (4) Release — volume at final position. (5) Repeat: narrow panel while dragging.
**Expected:** Slider grab is maintained across reparent; valueChanged fires continuously; no crash.
**Why human:** pytest-qt's programmatic slider events do not exercise the OS-level Wayland mouse-grab-during-resize path (compositor coalescing). VALIDATION.md Manual row 1.

#### 3. Compact-Toggle Icon Flip Across Reparent

**Test:** (1) In very-narrow state (cluster on row 3), click compact-toggle. Sidebar should hide; icon should flip to `sidebar-show-symbolic`. (2) Click again — sidebar reappears, icon flips back to `sidebar-hide-symbolic`, tooltip cycles correctly between "Hide stations (Ctrl+B)" and "Show stations (Ctrl+B)".
**Expected:** Icon and tooltip correct on both row 1 and wrap row; _on_compact_btn_toggled is the sole icon-flip handler (reflow helper never touches the icon).
**Why human:** Visual confirmation of icon rendering in both row positions. VALIDATION.md Manual row 3.

#### 4. No Flicker on Splitter-Drag Through Thresholds

**Test:** Drag main-window splitter slowly across BOTH reflow thresholds (picker threshold ~672px and volume-cluster threshold). Watch for visual flicker, layout-reshuffle artifacts, or transient overlap. Drag rapidly back and forth several times.
**Expected:** Smooth transition in both directions; no accumulated layout corruption; no ghost widgets at old positions.
**Why human:** Flicker budget (<=16ms reparent per UI-SPEC) cannot be measured reliably in pytest-qt. Wayland compositor coalescing effects only observable on live hardware. VALIDATION.md Manual row 4.
