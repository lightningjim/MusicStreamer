# Phase 72.1: Stream-selector dropdown overlap persists in fullscreen mode — Verification Report

**Phase Goal:** Width-responsive reflow of the now-playing controls row. When the panel narrows below the row-1-with-picker minimum AND a multi-stream station is selected, reparent `stream_combo` from the existing `controls` (QHBoxLayout, row 1) into a new `controls_row2` (QHBoxLayout) underneath. Picker stretches to fill row 2; reverts to its 140px-min Preferred sizing in row 1. Trigger is panel width (not compact-mode). Single-stream stations stay one-row at all widths.
